Sonic Pi Funding

We rely on you for your help...

The ongoing development of Sonic Pi has only been possible thanks to generous donations.
Initial funding was provided by the Broadcom Foundation and the last few years have been kindly funded by donations from the Raspberry Pi Foundation.

However, we really need your help to keep the project alive.
