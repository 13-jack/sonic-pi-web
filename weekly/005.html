Welcome to Sonic Pi Weekly #5

Hello Sonic Pioneers! Hopefully you're all settling down well into this new year. I've been working hard on some new features which will be part of (the very soon to be released) v2.3. One of the most exciting is a new flanger FX which was a blast to build. I've also started to get bookings for a number of gigs/assemblies/workshops so I've been practicing my live coding socks off in preparation! My latest collaboration is Poly Core, with Ben Smith, a fantastic guitarist that worked with me on the Sonic Pi: Live & Coding project. I'm constantly surprised by how much fun it is to jam Sonic Pi alongside a guitar. We're currently working on some new material, so watch this space...

Happy Live Coding!

Sam Aaron

Bett Stand Schedule | Raspberry Pi

For those educators out there, I'm sure most of you will be aware that the Bett Show is taking place this week. The most exciting thing about the Bett Show is that Raspberry Pi have a stand there which will feature no less than four Sonic Pi workshops. Three willl be delivered by myself and one by the fabulous Robin Newman.

  • Weds: 11:00, 15:30 - Sam Aaron: Audible Computing with Sonic Pi
  • Thurs: 12:00 - Sam Aaron: Audible Computing with Sonic Pi
  • Sat: 10:00 - Robin Newman: Sonic Pi: your imagination is the limit!

Of course, there are many more exciting things happening on the Raspberry Pi stand. Take a look at the full schedule:
